1Day 1: Ha Noi – Cai Rong port – Thanh Lan islet

Morning:

Pick up customers at the revenue to depart to Van Don.

Have breakfast in Sao Do, Hai Duong (personal expense).

Stop in Cai Rong port and have lunch in the restaurant then get on the cruise ship to Thanh Lan –Co To island. We go across Bai Tu Long and Thanh Lan – Co To, stop at Thanh Lan commune then take the room for rest.

Afternoon:

The car will pick you up to go to Hai Quan beach for relax and bath.

We can take some nice photos here and then capture the sunset in the island.

Night:

After dinner in the restaurant, we have time to free walk down on the beach.

Stay in Thanh Lan for night.

2Day 2: Thanh Lan Exploration

Morning:

Having breakfast

Trekking through a rice paddy and a primeval forest to reach C6 beach. Ocean here has the color of emerald with the spreading sand – banks alternate Pealit cliffs which are so impressed with many colours.

Afternoon:

Medium trekking for about 30 – 40 minutes, go through an impoundment and a primeval forest to reach Tam Chau- the most beautiful and primitive beach in the islet. You can freely have a bath and take photos in the pure water, see the coral reefs and the small islets near there.

Late afternoon, if you would like to see the dusk in Co To, you can go to Ang-ten beach. (extra fee for car: 50.000 VND/pax).

19h30: enjoy BBQ.

Stay in Thanh Lan for night.

3Day 3: Thanh lan – CoTo – Cai Rong – Ha Noi

Morning:

Having breakfast and then check out to go to the port.

Get on the cruise to come to Co To (around 30 minutes).

Seeing the love road, Ho Chi Minh statue and have a bath in the beach of the town.

Seeing Mong Rong stone bank, My bridge, Ong Minh cape which are the most beautiful scenery of Co To island.

Then you will see lighthouse. The trekking will be very interesting to reach the peak of the lighthouse. Whenever you reach the peak, you have chance to capture the whole sightseeing of the island with the mountain and ocean. Especially, you can join an special activities to take the unique photos.

Having lunch in Co To island restaurant.

Afternoon:

Get on the cruise to come back Van Don port and return Ha Noi.

20h: back to Ha Noi, the tour end successfully.
